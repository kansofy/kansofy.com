Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Kansofy Inc. ("we", "us", "our") operates the kansofy.com website. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you submit your email address through our "grow with us" form, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of submission
  • The page you submitted from (referrer URL)

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools on this page. We do not collect any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

How we use your data

Your email address is used solely to:

  • Add you to the BLOOM [MCP] waitlist
  • Send you updates about BLOOM [MCP] availability and features

We will not sell, share, or rent your email address to third parties.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure (D1 database) in the Western Europe region. Cloudflare maintains SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS compliance.

Your rights

You can request to:

  • See what data we hold about you
  • Have your data deleted
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To make a request, email privacy@kansofy.com.

Data retention

We retain your email address until you request its removal, or until the BLOOM [MCP] waitlist is no longer active.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
